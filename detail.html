Fun Facts about IT

1. The first computer virus

It was created in 1986 and was called "Brain." It was made by two brothers to protect their software from piracy.

2. Digital currency

Over 90% of the world's currency is digital. That means most of the money exists only in computers!

3. The first email

The first email ever sent was by Ray Tomlinson to himself in 1971. It was just a test message.

4. Google fun fact

Typing "Google" into Google used to break the search engine back in its early days.

5. The first 1GB hard drive

It was introduced in 1980, and it was the size of a refrigerator!

6. NASA's ancient tech

NASA still uses old computer systems from the 1970s for some of its space missions because they are reliable.
